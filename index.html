Final Year Project

Solar-Powered Cleaning Boat With Surveillance

About Project

The Solar Powered Cleaning Boat with Surveillance prototype is our answer to tackling water pollution in an eco-friendly way.
This product uses a conveyor system and Bluetooth control for efficient cleaning.
Powered by solar panels, it operates at a rate of 0.012 V/min under sunlight and is conveniently controllable within a 20m radius from the device.

My Role

In this project, my primary responsibility was overseeing the control of the product's movements.
Through extensive research and effort, I successfully enabled remote control functionality for the product, ensuring its movements could be effectively controlled.
